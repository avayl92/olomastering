Hey I'm Isabel.

I am a Mastering Engineer, Producer and DJ from Berlin. I am pursuing a deep passion for music ever since I first experienced the impact of sound waves entering my auricles. In 2020 I received my first class honour degree in sound engineering and opened up my mastering studio "Olo Mastering" in Berlin, where I also offer mixing duties.

Being an artist myself allows me to empathise with the needs and wishes of my clients. Creating a piece of music is an intensely personal and valuable thing in which someone has invested a great amount of time and energy. It is extremely important to me to treat your music with care and respect to let it reach its full potential.
