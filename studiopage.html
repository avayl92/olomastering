Olo Mastering is currently located in Kreuzberg, Berlin. The studio is designed as a “room-within-in-a-room” construction and professionally treated by an acoustician.

Analog Processing

Gainlab Audio Empress Tube Equalizer, Manley Variable Mu Compressor/Limiter, Revox B77 Tape Machine

Digital Processing

Airwindows, Fabfilter, Oeaksound, Ozone, Softube, Sonnox Oxford, TokyoDawn, Universal Audio, Weiss DS1-MK3, Wavelab

Monitoring

ATC SCM 25 A Pro, Neumann KH805 Subwoofer

Converters

Lynx Aurora(N) 8 TB3
