Monitoring: ATC SCM 25 A Pro, Focal Sub 6 Red Burr Ash
Digital Gear: Airwindows, Fabfilter, Oeaksound, Ozone, Softube, Sonnox Oxford, TokyoDawn, Universal Audio, Weiss DS1-MK3, Wavelab
